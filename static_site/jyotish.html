Jyotish

Sita Severson is the Director of Advanced Studies at the Soul of Yoga Institute, a yoga therapist, Ayurvedic practitioner, and mentor who turns Vedic tools into daily practice. She trained with the Chopra Center in yoga, ayurveda, meditation, and breathwork and has worked across clinics, campuses, and companies since 2007.

Sita uses Jyotish to speak in the voice of a person's soul and to write out their soul curriculum as she sees it through their chart. She uses Jyotish as a way of understanding one's gifts, karmic challenges, health concerns, and pathways that return people to the right relationship with their dharma.

Through careful chart interpretation, she helps individuals identify their natural strengths, areas where they are meant to grow, and karmic challenges that may appear as recurring patterns in relationships, career, health, or emotional well-being. Rather than viewing challenges as obstacles, she reframes them as opportunities for awareness, healing, and transformation.

A key aspect of her work lies in health and energy insights, where Jyotish is used to understand underlying imbalances, stress patterns, and psychosomatic influences that may manifest physically or emotionally. This perspective supports proactive self-care and alignment, complementing modern wellness approaches without replacing medical guidance.

At the heart of her practice is the concept of dharma — one’s true path and purpose. She guides individuals toward choices and timings that help restore alignment with their authentic nature, values, and life direction. Her readings are intended to bring clarity during periods of confusion, transition, or stagnation, helping people reconnect with meaning, inner stability, and right action.
